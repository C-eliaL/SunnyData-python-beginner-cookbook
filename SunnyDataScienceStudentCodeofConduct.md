# 【桑尼資料科學 Sunny Data Science】學員上課公約 / Sunny Data Science — Student Code of Conduct

歡迎加入桑尼資料科學的學習行列！為了確保每位學員都能享有最高品質的學習環境與權益，並維護智慧財產權，請詳閱並遵守以下公約。您的參與即代表同意本公約之所有條款。

Welcome to Sunny Data Science! To ensure every learner enjoys the highest-quality learning environment and to protect intellectual property, please read and comply with the following code of conduct. Your participation indicates your agreement to all terms stated in this document.

---

## 一、 課堂規範 (Classroom Etiquette)

由於我們是成人教育場域，我們重視彼此的學習時間與專注力。
Since we are in an adult education setting, we value each other's learning time and focus.

- 準時出席：請於課程開始前 5-10 分鐘進入教室（或線上會議室），測試設備以確保課程準時開始。遲到超過 15 分鐘者，講師有權不予單獨補述前面的內容。
**Punctual Attendance**: Please enter the classroom (or online meeting room) 5–10 minutes before the class begins and test your equipment to ensure a smooth start. If you are more than 15 minutes late, the instructor has the right not to repeat earlier content individually.
- 設備與環境：
**Equipment and Environment**:
    - 實體課：請將手機轉為靜音或震動。若需緊急通話，請至教室外進行。
    **In-person class**: Please switch your mobile phone to silent or vibration mode. For urgent calls, please step outside the classroom.
    - 線上課：請確保網路穩定，並在非發言時間將麥克風設為靜音，以免背景雜音干擾他人。
    **Online class**: Please ensure a stable internet connection. Keep your microphone muted when not speaking to avoid background noise for others.
- 尊重發言：鼓勵提問與交流，但請勿打斷講師教學節奏。建議利用聊天室文字提問，或於講師指定的「Q&A 時間」舉手發問。
**Respectful Communication**: Questions and interaction are encouraged, but please do not interrupt the instructor’s teaching flow. It is recommended to use the chatbox for questions or raise your hand during the instructor’s designated Q&A time.
- 錄音錄影限制：為保障講師隱私及課程版權，嚴禁學員私自進行全、或是部分課程的錄音、錄影或截圖直播。
**Recording Restrictions**: To protect the instructor’s privacy and course copyrights, students are strictly prohibited from conducting full or partial audio recording, video recording, screen capture, or livestreaming of any part of the course.

## 二、 課後規範 (Post-Class Guidelines)

學習的成效取決於課後的練習與累積。
The effectiveness of learning depends on post-class practice and accumulation.

- 作業繳交：若課程包含作業或專案，請務必於指定期限內透過指定平台繳交。遲交作業將可能影響您的結業證書領取資格或講師批改的優先順序。
**Assignment Submission**: If the course includes assignments or projects, please submit them through the designated platform within the specified deadline. Late submissions may affect your eligibility for obtaining the course completion certificate or the instructor’s grading priority.
- 缺席與補課：
**Absence and Make-up Policy**:
    - 實體課程：為確保教學品質與進度，實體課程缺席一律不提供補課，請學員務必自行安排時間準時出席。
    **In-person class**: To ensure the quality and progress of instruction, make-up classes will not be provided for in-person absences. Students are expected to manage their time and attend punctually.
    - 線上課程：若因故缺席，請依照本單位規定的流程觀看回放影片（需於課後指定期限內完成）。
    **Online class** : If you are absent for any reason, please follow our official procedures to watch the replay video within the specified period after the class.
- 學習態度：成人學習強調自主性，建議課後主動複習程式碼與講義，並嘗試將所學應用於實際工作場景中。
**Learning Attitude**: Adult learning emphasizes self-motivation. It is recommended to review the code, notes, and slides after class and try to apply what you have learned to real work scenarios.

## 三、 教材授權與智慧財產權 (Intellectual Property Rights)

這是本公約最嚴格執行的部分，請務必嚴格遵守。
This is the most strictly enforced part of this code of conduct; please comply strictly.

- 授權範圍：桑尼資料科學 (Sunny Data Science) 提供之所有教材（包含但不限於簡報 PDF、程式碼範例、數據集、影音檔、講義），僅供報名本課程之學員「個人學習使用」。
**Scope of Authorization**: All teaching materials provided by Sunny Data Science (including but not limited to PDF slides, code samples, datasets, videos, and course notes) are authorized for personal learning use only by the enrolled student.
- 禁止外流：
**Prohibitions on Distribution**:
    - 嚴禁將教材上傳至任何公開或私密網路平台（如 GitHub Public Repo、個人部落格、Google Drive 分享連結、公司內部網路等）。
    You are strictly prohibited from uploading teaching materials to any public or private online platform (e.g., GitHub public repo, personal blog, Google Drive shared link, company intranet, etc.).
    - 嚴禁將教材轉售、轉贈、列印販售或作為商業培訓用途。
    You are strictly prohibited from reselling, giving away, printing for sale, or using materials for commercial training purposes.
    - 嚴禁與非學員共用帳號觀看課程影片。
    You are strictly prohibited from sharing account access with non-students to watch course videos.
- 引用規範：
**Citation Rules**:
    - 若您希望在個人的學習筆記或作品集中引用部分程式碼或觀念，必須註明出處為「桑尼資料科學 Sunny Data Science」。
    If you wish to reference part of the course materials in your personal notes or portfolio, you must credit the source as: “Sunny Data Science”.
    - 引用比例不得超過原作的 2%。
    The citation must not exceed 2% of the original material.
    - 若有商業用途、學術研究或其他特殊需求，請務必事先向官方洽談申請授權，經書面同意後方可使用。
    For commercial use, academic research, or any special purposes, please contact the official team to obtain written authorization before use.

## 四、 違規處理與罰則 (Violations & Penalties)

若經查證學員違反上述公約，桑尼資料科學將採取以下措施：
If it is verified that a student has violated the above code of conduct, Sunny Data Science will take the following measures:

- 初次違規 / 輕微違規：給予口頭警告，並要求立即修正行為（如撤下侵權內容、停止干擾課堂等）。
**First-time / Minor Violations**: A verbal warning will be issued, and the student must immediately correct their behavior (such as removing infringing content or ceasing disruptive behavior).
- 嚴重違規處理：
**Serious Violations**:
    - 若學員行為嚴重影響上課秩序（如持續干擾教學、言語霸凌、不服從講師指導等），講師或助教有權立即請該名學員離開課堂。
    If a student seriously disrupts the class (e.g., persistently interrupting teaching, verbal bullying, refusing instructor guidance), the instructor or TA reserves the right to ask the student to leave the classroom immediately.
    - 被請離之學員，不進行退費及補課。
    The student will not receive any refund or make-up sessions.
- 智慧財產權侵權：
**Intellectual Property Infringement**:
    - 若涉及教材惡意大量外流或轉售圖利，除終止學員權益且不予退費外，本單位將依據《著作權法》及相關法律規定，追究民事賠償責任及刑事責任。
    If malicious large-scale distribution or resale of teaching materials is verified, the student’s course rights will be terminated immediately with no refund. Sunny Data Science will pursue civil compensation and criminal liability in accordance with the Copyright Act and related laws.
